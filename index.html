About HYDROGENIX

NOT JUST A
COMPETITION

HYDROGENIX 2026 is a premier national innovation platform jointly organized by Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU), Kanpur and Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), Gorakhpur.

Dedicated to accelerating India's transition toward a Green Hydrogen Economy, HYDROGENIX fosters breakthrough ideas in engineering, research, sustainability, and commercialization.

It is a research-driven innovation journey that transforms ideas into scalable solutions for the energy challenges of tomorrow — aligned with India's National Hydrogen Mission.

Our Vision
Create a national ecosystem for students, researchers & industry leaders to collaborate on sustainable hydrogen technologies.
The Mission
Empower young innovators to lead India's clean energy revolution through practical, research-backed solutions.
Organized By

TWO PILLARS OF
EXCELLENCE

HBTU
Harcourt Butler Technical University
📍 Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh
Established in 1921, HBTU has been at the forefront of technological education for over a century. Originally founded as the Government Technological Institute, UP, it spreads across two campuses — East (77 acres) and West (271 acres) — offering a world-class environment for student development in chemical, oil, and engineering disciplines.
Est. 1921 — 100+ Years of Excellence
MMMUT
Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology
📍 Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh
Established in 2013, MMMUT was reconstituted from Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College (est. 1962). Located on the Gorakhpur-Deoria road, the university offers diverse programs across Civil, Chemical, Computer Science, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering, along with MCA, MBA, M.Tech, M.Sc., and Ph.D. programs.
Est. 2013 — 54+ Batches of Engineers
Innovation Areas

CHOOSE YOUR
TRACK

Participants must align their projects with one of six broad innovation themes spanning the full green hydrogen value chain.

01
⚡
Hydrogen Production
  • Renewable electrolysis systems
  • Solar / wind powered generation
  • Biomass or waste-to-hydrogen
  • Catalyst & electrode innovation
  • Process optimization & scale-up
02
🔋
Hydrogen Utilization
  • Fuel cell power systems
  • Industrial decarbonization
  • Power-to-X fuels (ammonia/methanol)
  • Hydrogen blending in gas networks
  • Stationary energy storage
03
🏗️
Hydrogen Storage
  • Compressed gas storage
  • Cryogenic liquid hydrogen
  • Solid-state storage (metal hydrides)
  • Chemical carriers (LOHC/ammonia)
  • Storage safety & thermal management
04
🚛
Hydrogen Transport
  • Pipeline infrastructure design
  • Tube trailers & cylinders
  • Liquid hydrogen shipping
  • Ammonia-based transport
  • Smart monitoring & IoT tracking
05
🛡️
Hydrogen Safety
  • Leak detection systems
  • Explosion risk mitigation
  • Codes & standards compliance
  • Risk modeling (HAZOP/QRA)
  • Emergency response planning
06
💹
Hydrogen Economy
  • Techno-economic analysis
  • Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)
  • Policy & National Hydrogen Mission
  • Supply chain & market models
  • Startup & commercialization ideas
